My name is Carlos Hinojosa and I’m a final-year Ph.D Student in Computer Science with seven years of progressive experience in scientific research and software development.

I received the B. Sc. and M. Sc. degrees in Computer Science from Universidad Industrial de Santander, Colombia, in 2015 and 2018, respectively. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. in computer science at Universidad Industrial de Santander. My main research areas are computational imaging, optical code design, compressive imaging, computer vision and sparse representation.
